Optimal Nutrients Prediction

In this model, we provide various inputs like the type of plant to be grown and the environmental conditions of the facility to predict the minimal quantities of minerals like K, P and S which must be provided for its optimal growth. In real time, it can accept data from various sensors, decript it, preprocess it and predict the optimal composition of nutrients required. Here is a simple demonstration.

Diseased Crop Classification

In this model, we provide the image input and based on various spacial features predict whether the crop is healthy or not. Unhealthy Crops may hamper the productivity of the facility. In real time, it can analyse various frames from footage taken by robotic vision techniques, preprocess it and classify diseased crops which can later be removed. Here is its simple demonstration.
